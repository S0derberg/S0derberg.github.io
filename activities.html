Habonim Dror Camp Gilboa

Habonim Dror is a Jewish youth movement focused on educating and empowering kid while they have fun and create lifelong bonds. I spent all my summers from 2005 to 2014 at the Big Bear based summer camp; first as a camper, then as a counselor. Throughout the year we have monthly events for the kids to see each other, continue learning, and sometimes act on the movement's values. Two years ago I was the regional director of activities for the Bay Area, so I was in charge of the staff that planned and ran all the activities. Know any Jewish kids looking for an amazing summer experience? Send them to our website!
Tennis at Cal

I've been playing tennis since I was twelve years old. In my freshman year, I joined Tennis at Cal (TAC), UC Berkeley's intramural tennis team. I practiced/played with them for six of my semesters in college.
JEWSE (Jews in Science and Engineering)

This is a group that I was involved with for all four years of my undergrad experience; JEWSE is a club for Jews (and non-Jews too) that major in the sciences or engineering (others welcome too) who like to hang out and build cool things. I was vice president of the club for a year and then president for my senior year. Check the club out here!
